Welcome.

Welcome to my first website. Here you will find a collection of my favourite pieces including arts, photography and graphic design. I'm a passionate creator and an endless dreamer.

I was born and raised in Rome (Italy) from a Chinese family. Naturally, I developed a strong desire to see and travel the world. Starting from University and throughtout my professional career I lived and visited many countries. I'm always facinated by the way of living of different cultures and the nature surrounding us.

As human being we achieved so much, but we can do so much more. We can make this world a better place for future generations. I want to inspire you to do what makes you happy and make others' life better throught design.
